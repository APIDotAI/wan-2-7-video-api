# Prompt examples for Wan 2.7 Video

These prompts are starting points for testing Wan 2.7 Video through APIDot. Adjust brand names, subject details, source URLs, and safety rules before production use.

## 1. wan2.7-text-to-video

A cinematic tracking shot of a glass greenhouse at sunrise, soft light, slow camera movement.

## 2. wan2.7-image-to-video

Animate the product with a slow dolly-in and realistic reflections.

## 3. wan2.7-reference-to-video

Create a polished product hero video using the references for subject identity and lighting style.

## 4. wan2.7-edit-video

Restyle the source clip as a premium winter sports commercial while keeping the original motion.

## Production prompt notes

- Store prompt versions together with task_id, model, user/job owner, and request payload.
- Keep source media URLs reachable long enough for APIDot to fetch them.
- Be explicit about camera motion, subject continuity, scene pacing, audio intent, duration, resolution, and aspect ratio.
- Do not log prompts or source media URLs if they may contain private customer data.
